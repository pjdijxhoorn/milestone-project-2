Welcome to this site. This site is meant for coaches and managers of fieldhockey teams. However it may be used for other sports as well. Under this text you can create, alter and remove a team. When you go to the timer, by using the timer button above you will be able to individually time all the players for their total playing time and how long they are being benched. After timing the players you can find a overview of the played time on the results page.

When you made a player-list you will see blocks with names beneath this text. These blocks can be dragged in and out the field. You can use them to keep track of the positions of the players in the field.

The buttons on the bottom start, stop and reset the main timer as well as all the personal timers. The green toggle-buttons switch the timer from bench to field. After switching from bench to field the bench timer will be reset so you can see how long the break of that player was. The field time will is the overall time the player has played.
